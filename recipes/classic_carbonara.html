Classic Carbonara

The Classic Carbonara: No cream, just bacon, egg, and cheese.

Seasoned and lightly crispy. A thing of beauty, just like you are. Gently serve with pepper

Ingredients:

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Diced Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. The number of eggs depends on the number of servings. Add an additional egg and then scramble them.
  2. Grate your cheese.
  3. Prepare your pasta by cooking it in boiling water.
  4. Simultaneously, cook your diced bacon to a crisp.
  5. Add your scrambled eggs into your bacon but do not let them fully cooked. Add your cheese also and season with pepper.
  6. Once your pasta is cooked, add them to the mix. Add an amount of pasta water to your liking.
  7. Season with pepper and serve
