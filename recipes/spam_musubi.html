Spam Musubi

Inexpensive & Portable

Spam musubi is a snack and lunch food composed of a slice of grilled Spam sandwiched either in between or on top of a block of rice, wrapped together with nori in the tradition of Japanese onigiri.

Ingredients:

  • Seeweed Wrap
  • Rice shaped in blocks
  • Scrambled eggs
  • Spam

Steps:

  1. Cook or reuse leftover rice and shape them into blocks
  2. Cook your spam and scramble them eggs
  3. Season your rice, spam, & eggs with whatever
  4. Wrap all of them up with the seeweed wrap
  5. Enjoy your spam musubi
